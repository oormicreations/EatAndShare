Eat and Share is an Android app to quickly calculate the restaurant bill shares for each of your friends if you wish to split the bill or pay contributions equally.

Enter the bill amount and number of people to instantly get the contributions of each.

If you wish to pay the tip, enter a % (e.g. 10% of bill amount). The tip amount is calculated and added to the bill amount, also the contributions are updated.

You can also directly enter the tip amount, or even the total bill amount, if you wish to round it off. The extra amount gets added to the tip.

This app is open source and free. Please share :)
